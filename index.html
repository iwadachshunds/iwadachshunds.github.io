About Me

I am a student (at Concord High School), leader, runner, and study guide creator. I do many other things as well: WorldQuest (2 time state champion, 6th in the USA!), Academic Bowl, Ultimate, Cross Country, Track, TSA, Volunteering.... I am always interested in learning about new facts, ideas, and people. Feel free to contact me using the information at the bottom of the website & be sure to check out the rest of the site!

Leadership

I have always enjoyed helping groups run smoothly, and it is my goal to improve any group in which I participate. I earned my official first leadership position as NJHS Treasurer. From there, I became my chapter's TSA secretary and treasurer, and I went on to become the Delaware TSA State Treasurer! This year, I was re-elected to Delaware TSA State Office as the 2016-17 State Reporter. I pledge to continue to use my ability to lead for good, and I will continue to strive to become an ever-better leader.

Study Guides

I make and have made many study guides over the years, starting with US History in eighth grade, and up through AP Biology and AP World History this year. Check out this site for review guides, whether you're in AP, Honors, or CP classes. I will also document the strategies I use when studying for tests.
